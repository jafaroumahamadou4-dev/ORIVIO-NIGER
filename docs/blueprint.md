# **App Name**: ORIVIO Hub

## Core Features:

- Informational Landing Page: Showcase ORIVIO's mission, services, and impact using concise text and impactful visuals, targeting youth in West Africa with a mobile-first approach.
- Opportunity Board: A categorized directory for employment, scholarships, entrepreneurship, and mentorship opportunities with a card-based design for clear presentation.
- Partnerships Page: Highlight ORIVIO's collaborators with a call-to-action encouraging new partnerships.
- Email Subscription: Capture user emails and profile information for updates on new opportunities through a clear and concise form.
- Subscription Management: Store and manage user email subscriptions to notify users about new opportunities.
- AI-Powered Opportunity Summaries: Employ an AI tool that provides summaries of opportunities posted, including job roles, mentorships and scholarships, distilling them down to their essential components.
- Contact and Support Form: Users can send messages using a form to request support, additional information, or clarify the services offered by Orivio.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Midnight Blue (#003366), providing a professional and trustworthy feel.
- Secondary color: White, offering a clean and readable layout for largely informational content.
- Accent color: Orange (#FF8C00), drawing the eye and giving calls to action emphasis without being intrusive, while coordinating with violet.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, for a blend of modernity and warmth, suited to both headlines and body text.
- Mobile-first, ensuring a seamless experience on smartphones.  Clean and intuitive navigation for ease of use.
- Simple and recognizable icons to represent various services and target groups.  Consistent style to enhance usability.
- Subtle animations for transitions and button hovers. Improve engagement without overwhelming the user.
